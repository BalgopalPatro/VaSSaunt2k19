VaSSaUnt

ABOUT

VaSSaUnt, the cultural three days fest of Veer Surendra Sai University, Burla, is one of the most awaited inter college cultural events in the State. VSSUT, having the repute of being the oldest premiere engineering institute of the state, pulls a plethora of participants from the State as well as from outside the state. With more than a hundred evens and 3 pronites, we aim at making this event a lifetime memory for you.
